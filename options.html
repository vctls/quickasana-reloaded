How to get your personal access token

  • Click here
  • Click "+ Create new token"
  • Type "Quick Asana" in the "Token name" field
  • Check the box next to "I agree to the API terms and conditions"
  • Click "Create token"
  • Click "Copy"
  • Return to this tab
  • Paste the token into the field below
